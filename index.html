Natasja Saad

The artist that you've all heard, but never knew.

Bio

Saad, the daughter of Danish photographer Kirstine Saad, and a Sudanese father. She started singing and DJ'ing at the age of 13 in Copenhagen, No Name Requested. During that period, she also performed along with Queen Latifah and gained popularity in Jamaica.

In 1998, she trained to be a professional horse jockey and later became the first female jockey to win a horse race in Sudan. In 2006 she won the reggae/Irie FM Big Break Contest in Jamaica, which gave her a new start and a big boost in her music career.

In 2007 Natasja career as a singer was looking very promising, she was starting to gain international attention and had just finished recording her latest album. Unfortunately on June 24th, 2007, Natasja was killed in a car accident in Kingston Town, Jamaica.
